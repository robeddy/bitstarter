The Keytar is a computer keyboard shaped like a guitar. The keys face up toward the keytarist, and appear upsidedown to spectators and office groupies, further bending their minds while you perform your spreadsheet sorcery and meeting minutes magic.

The keytar may increase your words-per-minute, or it won't. We can only guarantee that you've never kicked it in your cube quite like this. The alphabet keys are were you'd expect them. All other keys are where they should've been all along. Who wants a whammy bar when you can have a space bar? [huh?] The keytar is scientifically designed for the modern office artist.

The Keytar was invented by famed keytarist and locally famous designer, Habermagic. The Keytar by Habermagic comes in one of two models: Clapton (Righty) or Hendrix (Lefty). Keytar skins, labels and other accessories will be available soon for a very low price. Become a part of the movement and help us rock office parks and corporate high rises world-wide.
